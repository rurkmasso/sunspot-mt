About Sunspot — Malta's beach booking platform

Sunspot is Malta's only dedicated booking platform for beach clubs, lidos and rooftop pools — built in Valletta, run by people who actually use the beaches they sell.

Built in Valletta by people who actually use the beaches

Anyone who has lived a summer in Malta knows the routine: you wake up early, drive to a beach, park badly, walk to the sunbed kiosk, and find out it's full. By 9am. Every weekend.

The Maltese islands have more than 40 commercial beach clubs and rooftop pools — but very few of them sell sunbeds online. We started Sunspot to fix exactly that: one place where you can see what's actually free today, click your spot, pay, and turn up.

How it works

  1. Live availability.Each operator updates inventory in their own dashboard. What you see is what's bookable.
  2. Pick the actual spot.Not just "a sunbed" — the exact one on the layout. Front row, poolside, in the shade — your call.
  3. Pay securely.Card, Apple Pay, Google Pay. We hold the booking; the operator gets paid out weekly.
  4. QR check-in.Show your booking QR at the entrance. No paper, no queue.
  5. Free cancellation.Up to 24 hours before. After that, the operator's policy applies.

Where we operate

Sunspot launched with 16 partner venues spanning Malta, Gozo and Comino:

  • Floating: Noma Island
  • Rooftop pools: FLO Skypool, Café del Mar, Twenty Two
  • Sliema lidos: Aqualuna, 1926 La Plage, Manta
  • Sandy bays: Mellieħa, Għajn Tuffieħa, Ramla, Paradise Bay, Pretty Bay
  • Natural sites: St Peter's Pool, Blue Lagoon, Dwejra Inland Sea
  • Quiet Gozo: Xlendi, Ħondoq ir-Rummien

For operators

We're built differently from generic booking platforms. We're Malta-specific: we understand sea-state cancellations, off-season pricing, and the regulars-vs-tourists balance every operator manages. Our take is a flat 8% service fee— no listing fees, no monthly minimums, no exclusivity demands.
