How we write our guides

Every guide on Sunspot is written by someone who has been there in person. We pay our own admission fees, take our own photos, and revise the guide at the start of every season. We do not accept paid placements, comp visits, or "featured" slots.

If a venue's prices, vibe or service changes, we update the guide and stamp it with the new date. Each article shows when it was last reviewed.

If you spot something wrong, email editorial@sunspot.mt — corrections go out within a week.
