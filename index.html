Frequently asked questions

How much does it cost to book a sunbed in Malta?

Sunbeds across Malta and Gozo range from €15 (St Peter's Pool) to €50 (Comino's Blue Lagoon front row). Sunspot shows live, all-in pricing — no surprise fees at the gate.

Can I cancel my reservation?

Yes. All Sunspot bookings include free cancellation up to 24 hours before your reservation. After that, the operator's individual policy applies.

Do beach clubs in Malta require booking in advance?

From mid-June to early September most popular clubs (Comino Blue Lagoon, Għajn Tuffieħa, Ramla Bay) sell out by 9am. Booking ahead with Sunspot guarantees your spot.

What's the difference between a sunbed, a cabana and a VIP gazebo?

A sunbed is for one person. A cabana is a shaded structure for up to 4 people, usually with side curtains and a small table. A VIP gazebo seats 6 and includes amenities like a fruit platter, bottle service and reserved access.

Which is the best beach club in Gozo?

Ramla Red (red-sand bay, mythological Calypso cave) and Xlendi Cove (intimate inlet between cliffs) are crowd favourites. Browse all Gozo beach clubs above.

Is Sunspot affiliated with the beach clubs?

Sunspot is an independent booking marketplace. We partner directly with each operator so you get instant confirmation and the same prices you'd pay at the gate.

Run a beach club?

List your sunbeds on Sunspot in under an hour. We handle bookings, payments, no-shows and rebookings — you focus on guests.
