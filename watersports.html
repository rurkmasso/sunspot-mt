Before you book — read this.

Season

Watersports kiosks open from late April and close end of October. Outside those dates, scuba and kayak rentals continue but everything tow-pulled (jetski, wakeboard, banana) pauses for winter.

Wind & sea-state

The right activity depends on the wind. Mellieħa stays sheltered in north-west wind. The south coast (Marsaxlokk, St Peter's) is calm in north-east. Operators cancel for swell over 1.5m — you'll be refunded automatically.

Licences & age

Jetski rental over 30 min and wakeboard tow need a basic licence (€20 same-day card at the kiosk). Most rentals are 14+ with parental signature; the kid-friendly tube + pedalo + banana are 6+.

What to bring

Reef-safe sunscreen, a towel, water shoes for rocky entries, and €1–2 cash for the kiosk loo. Wetsuits, helmets, life-jackets, and gear are provided unless the listing says otherwise.

How payment works

Pay in full at booking. Free cancellation up to 24 hours before the slot. Operator weather cancellations are refunded automatically within 24 hours.

If you're nervous

Everything on this page has a calm version. Start with a try-dive, a pedalo, or a SUP rental in Spinola Bay. Don't book a 2.5-hour Comino jetski safari if you've never been on one.
