Before you book.

Season

Watersports kiosks open late April and close end of October. Scuba and kayak rentals run year-round.

Wind & sea

Mellieħa stays sheltered in NW wind. The south coast is calm in NE. Operators refund automatically when they cancel for swell.

Licences & age

Jetski over 30 min and wakeboard tow need a basic licence (€20 same-day card). Pedalo and banana are 6+.

What to bring

Reef-safe sunscreen, towel, water shoes for rocky entries. Wetsuits and gear are provided.

Payment

Full payment at booking. Free cancellation up to 24 hours before. Weather cancels are refunded within 24 hours.

If you're nervous

Start with a try-dive, a pedalo, or a SUP rental in Spinola Bay. Skip the 2.5-hour Comino jetski safari if it's your first time.
