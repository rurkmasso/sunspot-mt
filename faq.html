Bookings

How far in advance can I book?

Up to 60 days aheadfor most venues. Some operators open inventory weekly — if you can't see your dates, check back on Sunday evening when next week's slots typically go live.

Can I book for the same day?

Yes — same-day booking is available right up to 1 hour before the venue closes. Live availability is shown on each venue page.

Do I get to pick the exact sunbed?

Yes. Every Sunspot venue has a unique seatmap with each spot pickable individually. Front row, poolside, in the shade — you choose. No more "I asked for a quiet corner" surprises.

What's the difference between a sunbed, cabana and VIP gazebo?

Sunbed— one lounger with parasol. €10–40 depending on venue.
Cabana— a four-person semi-private setup with table service. €60–200.
VIP gazebo— six-person fully enclosed cabana with bottle service, private fridge, dedicated waiter. €200–400.

Is breakfast/lunch included?

Sunbed bookings: no, food is à la carte. Cabana bookings: usually a welcome drink + light bites; full menu at venue prices. VIP: usually includes a bottle and platter. Each venue's page lists what's included.

Can I bring my own food and drink?

At free public beaches(walk-on category): yes, of course.
At commercial venues(beach clubs, lidos, pool clubs): no, all venues prohibit outside food and drink. The operator's revenue depends on F&B sales — that's how sunbed prices stay reasonable.

How do I check in?

You'll get a QR code by email after booking. Show it at the venue entrance — no need to queue at the kiosk. Most venues scan it on the way in and you head straight to your spot.

Cancellation & refunds

What's the cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 24 hours beforeyour booking date. Full refund to your original payment method within 5 business days.

After the 24h window: the venue's own policy applies (usually 50% refund or full credit toward another date).

What if the venue cancels (weather / sea-state)?

Full refund, automatically processed within 24 hours. Plus a 10% credit on your next booking as a thank-you for the hassle.

Operators err on the safe side — if there's a swell over 1.5m or wind over 25kt, floating venues (Noma, Crystal Lagoon) cancel.

Can I change the date instead of cancelling?

Yes — date changes are free up to 24h before, subject to availability on the new date.

Why hasn't my refund arrived yet?

Stripe refunds typically appear in 3–5 business days on cards, 1–2 days on Revolut. If it's been over a week, email hello@sunspot.mtwith your booking ref and we'll trace it.

Weather & sea-state

What if the weather looks bad on my booking day?

You decide. If it's just overcast, your booking stands. If it's actually unsafe (high wind, swell, storm), the operator will cancel and we'll refund automatically.

For floating venues (Noma, Crystal Lagoon), check our homepage sea-state widget24h before — it shows wind/wave forecast and a colour-coded verdict.

Which beaches work in different winds?
  • Majjistral (NW wind):south coast stays calm — St Peter's Pool, Marsascala, Pretty Bay.
  • Grigal (NE wind):west coast is sheltered — Għajn Tuffieħa, Ġnejna, Fomm ir-Riħ.
  • Xlokk (SE wind):bad in the south; head north (Mellieha, Anchor Bay).

See our Living herepage for the full wind-by-beach guide.

When is the sea swimmable in Malta?

Comfortable swimming: June 1 to October 31. Peak sea temperature 26°C in August. November is the cut-off for most — sea drops to 19°C by month-end.

Pool clubs (heated): year-round.

Jellyfish — should I worry?

Late August/September brings mauve stinger jellies on north shores. Painful sting but not dangerous. Most lidos have vinegar bottlesat the lifeguard post. Comino and the south coast usually escape it.

Payment & fees

How does the 8% service fee work?

The sunbed price is exactly what the operator listed. Sunspot adds a flat 8% on top, shown clearly at checkout. That's how we keep the lights on. No subscription, no booking fee, no payment-processing surcharges.

Example: €25 sunbed + €2 Sunspot fee = €27 total. Operator gets €25, Sunspot gets €2.

What payment methods do you accept?

Card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), Apple Pay, Google Pay, SEPA Direct Debit (EU bank accounts), Revolut. All processed through Stripe — your card details never touch our servers.

Can I pay in GBP or USD?

Prices are in EUR (Malta's currency). The site shows prices in your chosen currency via the bottom-right switcher, but charging is always in EUR — your bank handles the conversion at its rate.

Do I get an invoice?

Yes — automatic VAT-compliant PDF invoice emailed after every booking. For business expenses, the operator's VAT number is on the invoice (each venue is invoiced separately).

Is there a Maltese tourist tax?

No — the Maltese €0.50/night Eco Contribution applies to accommodation only, not beach venues.

For operators

How do I list my venue on Sunspot?

Email operators@sunspot.mtor use the Become a partnerform. We'll send a short questionnaire, verify your business, and onboard you within 5 business days.

What does Sunspot charge operators?

Just the 8% service fee paid by the guest. Operators pay nothing— no listing fees, no monthly minimums, no exclusivity demands. You receive 100% of your listed price.

How and when am I paid?

Weekly Stripe Connect payouts directly to your IBAN, every Monday. You'll see a real-time balance and payout history in your operator dashboard.

Can I sell on my own channels too?

Yes — no exclusivity. Our API and CSV sync make it easy to keep inventory consistent across channels.

Tourists & visitors

I'm visiting Malta for X days — what should I book?

Use our interactive itinerary planner— tell it where you're staying, how long you have, what you like — it generates a day-by-day beach plan from our 112 venues.

I'm on a cruise — can I do a day trip?

Yes. The cruise port is in Valletta. Best near-port options: Cugò Gran Macina(Senglea, 10 min walk over the bridge), Aqualuna(Sliema, 20 min taxi), or Trabuxu rooftopfor sunset cocktails. Avoid Comino — the 90-min boat each way eats your day.

Do I need to print my QR code?

No — show it on your phone. We email it and add it to your My bookingspage so you can pull it up offline.

Is Malta safe for solo travellers / women travelling alone?

Very. Malta consistently ranks in the top 10 safest countries in Europe. Public transport runs late, taxis (Bolt, eCabs) are reliable, beach venues are family-environment until late. Standard common sense, nothing extra.

My account

Do I need to create an account to book?

No — guest checkout works. But signing up means saved cards, faster checkout next time, and all your bookings in one place.

How do I delete my account (GDPR)?

Account → Profile → Delete account. Anonymises your bookings (keeps the operator's revenue records intact) and removes your personal data within 30 days. You'll get an email confirmation.

Can I download my data?

Yes — Account → Profile → Download my data exports everything as JSON. Bookings, favourites, preferences, the lot.

What if I forgot my password?

Use the "Forgot password" link on the sign-in page— reset email arrives in under 60 seconds.
