How we keep this honest

  • Disclosed sponsorships always. If a guide article was sponsored, the line says so under the title. We don't hide it inside a "partner" footnote.
  • Editorial integrity over revenue. A featured slot doesn't buy a positive review. If the place is mediocre we'll say so. We've turned down sponsorships for that reason.
  • No fake scarcity. "1 slot left!" only appears when there really is. The whole point of building this was to fix the booking dishonesty.
  • Published rate card. The above is what we charge. We don't quote different prices to different operators based on what we think they'll pay.
